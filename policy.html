Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Passonetouch ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when accessing or using our website at https://www.passonetouch.com (the “Website”). By using our Website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Our primary purpose is to help students succeed in their exams through a series of tests. Your privacy and data security are very important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Personal Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name and email address.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a service or subscription, we collect billing information (such as payment card details) to process your payment securely.

2. Automatically Collected Information:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to gather information about your interactions with our Website, such as:

  • Your IP address.
  • Browser type and version.
  • The pages you visit and the time spent on the Website.
  • Device type and operating system.

For more details about cookies and how you can manage them, see the Cookies and Tracking section below.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation and Management: To create and manage your user account.
  • Service Delivery: To provide you access to tests, resources, and related services.
  • Payment Processing: To securely process your payments and send payment confirmations.
  • Communication: To send important updates, newsletters, or promotional content (you can opt out of marketing communications at any time).
  • Website Optimization: To analyze website performance and improve user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms of Use.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our Website by remembering your preferences and improving functionality. Cookies may also be used for analytical purposes, such as understanding how users interact with our Website.

Managing Cookies: You can modify your browser settings to refuse or delete cookies. However, this may limit certain functionalities of the Website.

Security of Your Information

We take data security seriously and use the following measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.
  • Secure Servers: Your information is stored on secure servers with limited access.

While we strive to protect your data, no system can guarantee complete security. Please safeguard your account credentials and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. However, we may disclose your information in the following circumstances:
